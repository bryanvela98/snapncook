How it works

  1. Take a photo Snap a clear picture of your fridge, pantry, or countertop ingredients. Recipes will be built only from what's visible + basic condiments (salt, pepper, oil, butter, flour, sugar, dried herbs). No extra grocery runs needed.
  2. Confirm ingredients Review what AI detected, remove anything wrong, and add anything that was missed or out of frame.
  3. Set your preferences Choose how many recipes you want (1–5), your maximum prep time, and any dietary restrictions.
  4. Get your recipes AI generates personalised recipes using only your ingredients, with full ingredient lists and step-by-step instructions.
